Projects

RoboCup Project - 2024

What's the RoboCup ? It's a robotics competition
where teams face eachother trying to complete
a set of challenges, and innovate in different
categories such as Soccer, Home, Rescue
and more.

In our case, our team of three students were
tasked to solve the Vision BlackOut challenge
in the Soccer SSL category. Usually the robots playing
are controlled by a camera system, but in this
challenge, the camera system is disabled and the
robots must play using only their sensors (camera).

First Bot - 2024

First Bot is a project made in collaboration with
the Einserb MatMeca Engeneering School. The goal
of the project is to create a robot that can
follow a line on the ground, and avoid obstacles.
In our case, we had two lines to follow, one red line
and one black line.

We had the necessary material given to us such as
a motor, a camera, a raspberry pi and more. Other
challenges we had to this project were kinematics
and odometry of the robot.
